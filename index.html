What is Website in a Day?

I've recently been inspired by Jennifer Dewalt who successfully made 180 websites in 180 days - awesome. Because my robust social life prevents me from making websites non-stop for 6 months, I will be challenging myself to see what I can produce in a day, several days a week. My goal is to be less self conscious about what I create in order to develop new skills. All code will be pushed to Github. Enjoy!
